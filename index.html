GRUB — ​​​​​​​Retro-Inspired Fast Food Branding



GRUB is a bold, fun, and retro-style fast food restaurant concept that brings a playful twist to classic American-style takeout. Specializing in burgers, pizzas, and sandwiches, GRUB is all about fast, flavorful food wrapped in a nostalgic design system that screams character and craveability.

The goal of this project was to craft a full brand identity system that reflects GRUB’s vintage soul, quality food with modern attitude — a balance between retro American diner aesthetics and current design trends.

Logo Design & Brand Identity ---------------- Designed by Dark X Design

Brand Vision

GRUB isn’t just food — it’s an experience. The brand is designed for fast food lovers who crave nostalgia with a side of hype. Think: classic flavors, throwback packaging, and a loud, memorable visual style. With bright colors, bold fonts, and a cheerful mascot, GRUB appeals to young, fun-loving foodies who appreciate both style and substance.

High quality visuals to bring the GRUB brand identity to life, showing how the brand lives in a real-world environment .

Logo Design & Typography



The GRUB logo uses a thick, retro-inspired custom typeface with smooth curves and chunky shapes. Designed to stand out on signage, packaging, and merchandise, the logo communicates approachability, energy, and boldness.


Type Style: Playful,rounded, and nostalgic

Legibility: Strong presence across all mediums — from wrappers to billboards

Brand Voice: Fun, youthful, expressive, and Bold





GRUB is a celebration of flavor, nostalgia, and energy. This brand identity was built to grab attention, tell a story, and leave a lasting impression — just like a perfect burger combo.
